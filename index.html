About Me

My name is Dr. Eduardo Ozuna. For the last 9 years I have been practicing pain management physician in the Austin/San Antonio area. I currently live in Lakeway, Texas, just outside of Austin.

Prior to becoming a physician, I was a Computer Programmer for the Texas State Legislative Budget Board for about 1 year. I have a BS in computer science and biology. Most recently I completed the UT Coding Bootcamp and developed more skills in full-stack developement.
